Homemade Spaghetti and Meat Sauce

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Beef broth
  • Canned mushrooms
  • Splash of wine
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Fresh garlic
  • Dried herbs
  • Bay leaves
  • Kosher salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Dried whole cloves

Directions

  1. Brown the ground beef and drain off any excess fat.
  2. Throw in the chopped celery, onion, and garlic and cook until the vegetables soften.
  3. Dump the rest of the homemade pasta sauce ingredients into the pot and bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Once the spaghetti sauce is boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer on the stove for 3 hours.
  5. Boil the spaghetti noodles according to package instructions.
  6. Serve.
